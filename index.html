Born in Rome and shaped by an insatiable curiosity, Gianfilippo's path began at La Sapienza University where he graduated with the highest honours in Aerospace Engineering. His early career at the European Space Agency instilled the precision-driven, systems-thinking mindset he carries into every business challenge today.

After earning an MBA from the Collège des Ingénieurs in Paris, he made a decisive pivot into luxury — joining LVMH with TAG Heuer, then building a distinguished career at Fendi across Rome, Tokyo, and Hong Kong. He subsequently joined Kering, leading Gucci's most strategically critical region — Greater China — before serving on the Executive Committee as VP Global Retail and President EMEA.

In 2022, he was appointed President & CEO of Alexander McQueen, where he led a bold strategic transformation to reposition the House for sustainable, long-term growth while preserving its radical creative identity.

At the heart of his leadership is a belief that great organisations are built on psychological safety, continuous learning, and the courage to take risks.
